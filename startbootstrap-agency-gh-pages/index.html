So, you want to know more...

Having worked for a website design agency in the sales team, being passionate about design and web development comes as second nature.

Being exposed to the industry and longing to design myself, I started learning basic HTML, CSS and JavaScript through online resources like codecademy and Youtube. Thriving on the combination of creativity and problem solving my interest in programming continued to grow. After months of self teaching I knew i wanted to pursue Front-End Development as a career and wanted to take my skills to the next level.

In October 2017 I joined the Development Professional Programme with Just IT. This self-funded course has been the first step in the journey towards becoming a front-end developer. Feeding my Front-End languages knowledge base and pushing my creative boundaries the course has left me highly motivated and hungry to learn more everyday.

WHACK-A-MOLE

Handcoded JavaScript Game

This fun game is my take on the arcade classic Whack-A-Mole, where the objective is to hit as many moles as possible with your cursor in the allotted time. To make things more interesting for the players I have created difficulty settings and a score counter using local storage!

Future Scope: To add an element of competition into the game, I would like to create a leaderboard where players scores are logged and they can try to beat the best highscore.

Technologies used : HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT, BOOTSTRAP.
